## Declaration

I, Belhalhali Mohamed Amine, hereby declare that:

1. I am the original and sole inventor of the invention described in this patent
   proposal titled **"Quantum-Enhanced Atmospheric Water Generator (Q-AWG)"**.

2. The invention was conceived and documented in Tlemcen, Algeria, in March 2026.

3. To the best of my knowledge and belief, the specific combination of:
   - Metal-Organic Framework MOF-303 doped with carbon quantum dots
   - Solar-thermal metamaterial desorption
   - Reinforcement learning cycle optimisation
   - Federated learning mesh coordination
   ...constitutes a new and non-obvious invention.

4. I am aware of prior art in the individual components (MOF water harvesting,
   quantum dot adsorption, solar AWG, RL for control systems) but am not aware
   of any prior art combining all four elements as described herein.

5. I understand my duty to disclose all information material to patentability
   and will do so throughout the prosecution process.

6. The simulation code in `/src/simulation/qawg_simulation.py` was written
   by me and implements the physics described in the patent claims.
