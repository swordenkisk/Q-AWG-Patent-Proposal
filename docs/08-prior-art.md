- **What it claims**: Solar-thermal AWG with silica gel
- **Our difference**: Uses silica gel (lower capacity), no MOF, no AI
- **Status**: Active, but our MOF+QD combination is distinct

### CN108126665A
- **What it claims**: QD-doped materials for adsorption
- **Our difference**: Heavy metal removal (Pb, Cd), not water vapour harvesting
- **Status**: Our water-specific application is novel
