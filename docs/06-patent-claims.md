# 6. 📋 Patent Claims

## Independent Claims

### Claim 1 — Core Device
An atmospheric water generator comprising:
(a) an adsorber bed containing a Metal-Organic Framework (MOF) doped with
    quantum dots, wherein the quantum dots enhance water vapour adsorption
    capacity by at least 25% compared to the undoped MOF;
(b) a solar-thermal desorber comprising a spectrally selective metamaterial
    absorber with solar absorptance greater than 90% and mid-IR emittance
    less than 10%;
(c) a condenser for collecting liquid water from desorbed vapour; and
(d) an artificial intelligence controller that optimises the timing of
    adsorption and desorption cycles based on predicted environmental conditions.

**Novelty**: First device combining all four elements (a)–(d).

---

### Claim 2 — Quantum Dot Type
The generator of Claim 1, wherein the quantum dots are carbon quantum dots
(CQDs) derived from biomass waste, having a size of 2–5 nm.

---

### Claim 3 — Green Synthesis Method
A method for preparing a MOF-QD composite adsorbent, comprising:
(a) preparing a suspension of carbon quantum dots from agricultural waste
    by hydrothermal carbonisation at 150–200°C;
(b) mixing the CQD suspension with aluminium salt and organic linker in water;
(c) synthesising the MOF-QD composite by one-pot hydrothermal treatment at
    100–150°C for 12–36 hours.

---

### Claim 4 — AI Optimisation Method
A method for controlling an atmospheric water generator, comprising:
(a) collecting real-time environmental data including temperature, humidity,
    and solar irradiance;
(b) predicting future humidity using a machine learning model;
(c) selecting operational mode (adsorb/desorb/idle) using a reinforcement
    learning agent trained to maximise water yield per unit energy.

---

### Claim 5 — Federated Mesh
A distributed system of atmospheric water generators of Claim 1, wherein
each unit:
(a) trains a local machine learning model using only its own sensor data;
(b) shares only model parameter updates (not raw data) with a central server;
(c) receives an aggregated improved model in return;
whereby collective intelligence improves without exposing private data.

---

### Claim 6 — Applications
Use of the generator of Claim 1 in off-grid communities, disaster relief
operations, military forward operating bases, or agricultural irrigation
in arid or semi-arid environments.

---

## Dependent Claims

**Claim 7**: The generator of Claim 1, wherein the MOF is selected from
aluminium fumarate (MOF-303), MIL-101(Cr), or UiO-66-NH₂.

**Claim 8**: The generator of Claim 1, wherein the solar-thermal desorber
comprises a photonic crystal selective absorber based on tungsten-doped TiN.

**Claim 9**: The generator of Claim 4, wherein the RL agent uses a Deep
Q-Network with dueling architecture to separately estimate state value and
action advantage.

**Claim 10**: The method of Claim 3, wherein the carbon quantum dots are
derived from date pits (Phoenix dactylifera), olive stones, or orange peel.
