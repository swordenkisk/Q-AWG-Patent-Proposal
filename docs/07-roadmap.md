# 7. 🛠️ Roadmap to Commercialisation

## Phase 1: Lab Prototype — 12 months | Budget: $150,000

**Objectives**:
- Synthesise MOF-QD composite and validate water uptake measurements
- Build single-unit proof-of-concept
- Characterise all thermal and adsorption properties
- Train first DQN agent on simulation environment

**Milestones**:
- [ ] Month 3  — CQD synthesis protocol from date pits finalised
- [ ] Month 6  — MOF-QD composite with ≥30% enhanced water uptake confirmed
- [ ] Month 9  — Working prototype achieving 1 L/day at 30% RH
- [ ] Month 12 — Performance validation report + provisional patent filed

**Key partners**: USTHB Algiers (materials), UABB Tlemcen (chemistry lab)

---

## Phase 2: Field Trials — 12 months | Budget: $400,000

**Locations**:
- Adrar (Sahara) — extreme low humidity, max solar
- Béjaïa (Mediterranean coast) — high humidity, moderate solar
- Biskra (semi-arid) — variable conditions, date palm agriculture

**Activities**:
- Deploy 10 units per location (30 total)
- 6 months continuous data collection
- Federated learning training across all units
- User feedback collection (Arabic/Tamazight interface)

**Milestones**:
- [ ] Month 18 — All 30 units deployed
- [ ] Month 21 — First federated model improvement measured
- [ ] Month 24 — ≥2 L/day average yield demonstrated at all sites

---

## Phase 3: IP Protection — 6 months | Budget: $75,000

- File PCT application (priority from provisional)
- National phase entries: US, EU, China, UAE
- Freedom to operate analysis for target markets
- Defensive publication strategy for minor improvements

---

## Phase 4: Scale-up — 18 months | Budget: $1,200,000

**Partnership Strategy**:
- Manufacturing: Algerian industrial partner or MENA water tech company
- Distribution: UNHCR / WFP for humanitarian market
- Commercial: SONELGAZ / ADE (Algeria) for national rural program

**Activities**:
- Design for manufacturing (DFM) — target $150/unit at 1,000 units/month
- Supply chain for AlCl₃, fumaric acid, date pits (all local)
- First commercial batch: 500 units
- NSF/ANSI 61 certification (water contact materials)

---

## Total Timeline: 3.5 years to commercial launch

```
Year 1: [Lab Prototype] [IP Filing]
Year 2: [Field Trials] [Federated Learning]
Year 3: [Scale-up] [First Sales]
Year 3.5: [Commercial Launch — Algeria + MENA]
```
